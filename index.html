Hello!

Last year, after a decade as a writer and college instructor, I changed course and embarked on a career in front-end web development. After months of studying on my own, I enrolled in a three-month web development immersive at General Assembly in Santa Monica. I emerged as a full-stack developer, but with a clear emphasis on the front end.

Since August 2016, I've been working at Gigmor, a Santa Monica startup connecting musicians with venues and bookers. Armed with just a few examples from an outside designer and some rough wireframes, I have developed a front-end look and experience that is clean, responsive and easy to use.

When not coding, I enjoy listening to podcasts, particularly about science and politics; visiting museums; reading long-form journalism; and collecting Legos.
