About

I am a newly minted full-stack web developer — including HTML, CSS, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, and MEAN stack — since completing an immersive program at General Assembly in Santa Monica. Prior to enrolling at GA, I worked for several years as a college instructor and writer, which taught me how to break down daunting problems and make complexity feel simple. I thrive on problem solving and learning new skills, so I am thrilled to be embarking on a career in web development.

When I'm not coding, I like to indulge my serious LEGO addiction, visit themeparks (Hogwarts at Universal Studios made my year), and read up on ridiculous Game of Thrones fan theories.
